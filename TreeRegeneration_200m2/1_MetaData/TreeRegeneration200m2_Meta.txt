TITLE OF STUDY: Tree Regeneration 200m2

ABSTRACT: The 200m2 plot was designed to assess tree regeneration at the scale of saplings 1.5-9.49cm dbh.  The 200m2 plots are centered on the center pin of each 25 x 25m quadrat. The boundary of the plot (radius = 7.98m) is determined by using a tape measure or marked rope. In 1984 160 quadrat-centered plots were measured.  In 1992 and 1997 100 plots on well- and excessively well-drained soil were measured. The 200m2 plots were too large to fit within gaps, so the 4m2 and 25m2 regeneration plots were used to assess regeneration differences between gaps and intact forest (see meta files for those datasets).
